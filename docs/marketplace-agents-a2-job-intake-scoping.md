# A2 Job Intake & Scoping (v1)

## Mission
Normalize job requirements into dispatch-ready scope packets.

## Required fields
- Timeline, budget range, deliverables, constraints, urgency.

## Rules-first behavior
- Enforce minimum scoping checklist by category.
- Add deterministic flags (`isUrgent`, `requiresPermit`, `needsSiteVisit`).
- If unresolved scope after two passes, route to A6 for expert clarification.

## AI/ML hook
- Category-specific scope inference and ambiguity scoring.
